The basics

What is bid writing?

Bid writing is the process of preparing a formal, structured written response to a tender opportunity, whether that is a Public Contract Notice, an Invitation to Tender (ITT), a Pre-Qualification Questionnaire (PQQ), or a framework application such as DOS 7 or G-Cloud.

When a public sector organisation, NHS trust, local authority or private company wants to procure a service, they issue a tender. To win that contract, suppliers must respond with a professionally written bid that demonstrates capability, value for money, social value and compliance with the buyer's requirements.

A poorly written bid, however strong your business, rarely wins. A professionally written bid, tailored to the buyer's scoring criteria and written with clear win themes, dramatically increases your chances of success.
