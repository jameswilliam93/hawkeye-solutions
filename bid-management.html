Understanding the service

What is bid management?

Bid writing gets the words right. Bid management makes sure everything else goes right too.

Bid management is the end-to-end coordination of your tendering activity. It covers identifying the right opportunities, assessing whether to bid, managing internal stakeholders and subject matter experts, tracking deadlines, quality assuring submissions and building the processes that make your bidding more consistent and more successful over time.

For many businesses, the biggest reason they lose contracts is not poor writing: it is poor process. Missed deadlines, incomplete attachments, inconsistent messaging, last-minute scrambles and key people unavailable when they are needed most. Bid management eliminates those risks.

Hawkeye Solutions provides bid management support for recruitment agencies and IT consultancies who are serious about winning contracts: either as a standalone service or alongside our bid writing and bid review offer.

What our bid management service covers

Our bid management service is built around one goal: making sure your bids go in on time, on brief and at the highest possible quality.

Opportunity Identification

We monitor live tender portals, frameworks and procurement notices daily across your target sectors. You hear about the right opportunities first: before your competitors do.

Not every tender is worth chasing. We assess each opportunity against your win probability, resource requirements and strategic fit: saving you time and money on bids you are unlikely to win.

Is this right for you?

Who needs bid management support?

Bid management support is most valuable for businesses in one of these situations:

You are bidding regularly but results are inconsistent

If you are putting in the effort but not seeing a consistent return, the problem is usually process rather than quality. A structured bid management approach identifies and fixes the gaps.

You have a major framework opportunity coming up

DOS 7, G-Cloud, NHS frameworks and other major procurement vehicles require coordinated effort across multiple workstreams. We make sure nothing falls through the cracks on the opportunities that matter most.

Your team is stretched and deadlines are being missed

Tender deadlines do not move. If your internal team does not have the capacity to manage the process properly alongside their day jobs, outsourcing bid management is the most cost-effective solution.

You are new to tendering and need a structure

If your business is entering the tendering market for the first time: particularly public sector procurement: bid management support gives you the framework, the processes and the expertise to compete properly from day one.

Common questions

Bid management services: frequently asked questions

What is the difference between bid writing and bid management?

Bid writing focuses on producing the written content of a tender response. Bid management is the broader coordination of the entire process: identifying opportunities, managing stakeholders, tracking deadlines, quality assuring submissions and improving performance over time. Many clients use both services together for a fully managed tendering operation.

Can you manage our bid pipeline on an ongoing basis?

Yes. We offer both project-based support for specific tenders and ongoing retainer arrangements where we manage your pipeline continuously. Retainer arrangements work particularly well for businesses that bid regularly and want consistent resource without the cost of an in-house bid team.

We already have internal bid writers: can you just manage the process?

Absolutely. Bid management as a standalone service works well for teams that have writing capability but need better process, coordination and quality assurance around it. We integrate with your existing team rather than replacing them.

How quickly can you get started on an urgent tender?

We can mobilise quickly for urgent opportunities. Contact us as soon as you identify a tender: even with a tight deadline, early engagement gives us the best chance of producing a strong submission. The earlier you come to us, the better the outcome.

Do you manage bids for public sector frameworks specifically?

Yes: public sector framework management is a core part of what we do. We have specific experience managing applications and call-off responses for NHS frameworks, DOS 7, G-Cloud and other Crown Commercial Service vehicles, as well as local authority and utilities procurement.

What sectors do you cover for bid management?

Our core specialisms are recruitment sector tendering and IT consultancy frameworks. We also support clients across public sector services, NHS procurement, utilities and professional services.
