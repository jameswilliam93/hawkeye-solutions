Our Services

Complete Bid Support

From a single bid review to full outsourced bid management, we tailor our support to what you need.

Bid Writing (Full Service)

We own the entire bid writing process on your behalf. This includes reviewing procurement documents, interviewing your subject matter experts, preparing answer plans, drafting all responses, managing the review cycle, and finalising documents for submission.

Ideal for organisations without in-house bid resource, or those with limited capacity facing a critical opportunity.

Bid Management

If you have writers but need experienced management, we take control of the process, coordinating your team, managing timelines, overseeing compliance, and ensuring everything comes together for a high-quality submission.

Ideal for businesses with in-house writers who need strategic oversight and process control.

Bid Review and Quality Check

A thorough, expert review of your draft submission. We assess compliance, quality of evidence, clarity of writing, and alignment with evaluation criteria, providing clear, actionable feedback to improve your score before submission.

Ideal for teams who write their own bids but want a professional second opinion before going live.

Bid Writing Training

Build lasting capability within your organisation. Our training programmes cover the full bid writing process, from finding the right opportunities to crafting high-scoring responses, and are tailored to recruitment and IT consultancy tenders.

Ideal for growing teams who want to develop in-house expertise and reduce reliance on external support.

Public sector procurement is governed by the Procurement Act 2023 and requires suppliers to respond to structured tender questions across quality, price, and social value.
